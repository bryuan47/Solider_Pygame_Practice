### 2Ô∏è‚É£ **Linear movement / constant velocity**

### 3Ô∏è‚É£ **Gravity simulation**

### 4Ô∏è‚É£ **Oscillating motion / sine wave**

### 5Ô∏è‚É£ **Acceleration / deceleration (ease-in, ease-out)**

### 6Ô∏è‚É£ **Friction / sliding (drift)**

* Objects slowly stop moving when you stop pressing keys

### 7Ô∏è‚É£ **Path following / lerp (linear interpolation)**

* Move an object smoothly from point A ‚Üí B

```python

```

### 8Ô∏è‚É£ **Bounce off walls / reflection**

* Enemy moves toward the player
